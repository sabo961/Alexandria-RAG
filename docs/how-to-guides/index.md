How-to guides are recipes that guide you through the steps to solve real-world problems. They assume you have basic knowledge of Alexandria and want to accomplish a specific goal.

## What Makes a How-To Guide?

How-to guides should:
- **Solve a specific problem** or accomplish a particular task
- **Provide practical steps** that work reliably
- **Be goal-oriented** and results-focused
- **Assume basic knowledge** of Alexandria
- **Not explain concepts** (link to Explanation docs instead)
