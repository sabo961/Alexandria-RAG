## What Makes a Tutorial?

Tutorials should:
- **Take the learner by the hand** through a series of steps
- **Help beginners get started** with Alexandria
- **Ensure success** by providing clear, tested instructions
- **Focus on learning**, not problem-solving
- **Provide a complete path** from zero to working system
